Welcome to the Land of Buddha and Mahavira

Bihar, the birthplace of Buddhism and Jainism, is a treasure trove of ancient wisdom, rich culture, and vibrant traditions. From the sacred Bodhi Tree in Bodh Gaya to the ancient ruins of Nalanda University, Bihar has been the cradle of knowledge and spirituality for centuries.
